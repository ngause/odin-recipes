Fruit & Yogurt Smoothie

Smoothie Recipe

This yummy recipe is sure to satisfy your cravings. Quickly toss the ingredients in the blinder and enjoy. Works with any fruit!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup strawberries
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 1-1/2 teaspoons white sugar
  • 1 teaspoon orange juice
  • 1 teaspoon milk

Steps

  1. Throw all that yummy stuff in a blender.
  2. Add in some ice.
  3. Blend that shirt until it's smooth.
  4. Drink it straight out of the pitcher!
